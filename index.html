Welcome to St. Eanswythe's Primary School

As the Headteacher I would like to welcome you to our community and invite you to explore the website which I hope will give you an impression of the caring ethos with which we seek to educate the children.

If you are looking for a school place in September 2026, please contact us to book a tour of our friendly school and its extensive grounds!

We pride ourselves on our rich curriculum, strong community links, and the exceptionally high standards of behavior and achievement our children demonstrate every day, all guided by our core Christian values.

I believe time spent in primary school sets the foundations upon which all future learning is built. We aim to provide a nurturing and creative community, where all children are taught to believe they can make a positive difference to the world.

I look forward to welcoming you.


Mrs Claire
Headteacher
